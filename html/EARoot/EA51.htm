6-08 Description of the schedule of observation. Note: Schedules are defined in terms of months covered, weekdays covered, hours and minutes covered during each day. A complete definition of a schedule requires specification of the temporalReportingInterval, and may require the specification of diurnalBaseTime.<br/>
