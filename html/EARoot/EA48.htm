A ResponsibleParty is an individual or organization accompanied by a timestamp indicating the time from which that responsibleParty is considered to be valid. If known, an end time may also be provided. In WIGOS, an ObservingFacility or Equipment may carry multiple responsibleParty which are valid over different periods of time.<br/>
